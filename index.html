Under the hood

The Skateboarding Dryness Engine

Every score in Skateable is powered by the SDE © a physics-based model built on the same thermodynamic equations meteorologists use to predict evaporation. Combined with Bayesian inference and community ground truth, it turns weather data into a dryness prediction you can trust.

Thermodynamics Vapour pressure deficit, solar radiation, and wind-driven evaporation
Surface physics Porosity, albedo, drainage, and sun exposure per spot
Community data Bayesian nudge from real skater reports calibrates the model
