What's new in Skateable

1.2 In review
  • Weather detail New
    Tap the weather section on the Dashboard to see 48h rain history and forecast, with hourly charts for precipitation, humidity, wind, and temperature.
  • Widget 2.0 Improved
    The home screen widget now shows descriptive condition labels and lets you pick which park to display directly from your home screen.
  • Session observations New
    Tag conditions when logging a session (wet, crowded, closed) and mark a visit as a quick observation without logging a full session. Recent community observations appear on the Dashboard.
  • Edit park details New
    Correct a park's surface type, sun exposure, or drainage directly from the Dashboard. Changes update the skateability score immediately.
  • Community notes New
    Approved observations from other skaters appear on the park Dashboard, so you know what to expect before you arrive.
  • Planned sessions New
    Schedule sessions up to a week ahead, rank your preferred parks, and get a notification when it's time to leave based on travel time and forecast conditions.
  • Proximity alerts New
    Get a nudge to log a session or observation when you arrive near a saved park. Configurable in Settings, with a cooldown to avoid repeat prompts.
  • Algorithm explanation New
    A new Settings section walks through exactly how the skateability score is calculated, covering weather inputs, surface factors, and your tolerance offset.
  • iCloud sync New
    Saved parks, favourites, settings, and planned sessions sync across your devices automatically. Everything restores from iCloud on a fresh install.
  • Skateboarding Dryness Engine (SDE) Improved
    A rebuilt scoring engine that produces more accurate skateability scores across different location types and conditions.

And more.

1.1 Current

April 2026

  • Location photos New
    Add photos to locations so others know what to expect before they visit.
  • Location types New
    Categorise spots: skatepark, street spot, bowl, DIY, and more.
  • Location attributes New
    Tag spots with surface material, ramp type, and other details that affect skateability.
  • Parking location New
    Pin a parking spot to any location, with address, walking distance, and optional notes. Tap to open navigation directly from the Dashboard.
  • Advanced filtering Improved
    Filter your saved locations by type, attributes, and current score.
  • Search Improved
    Faster, more relevant results when finding existing spots to save.
  • Map view Improved
    Pan and zoom the map to explore locations near any point. Expand to full-screen from the Dashboard.
  • Weather reliability Improved
    Scores stay available even when the primary weather provider is down, with automatic fallback to a secondary source.

And more.
