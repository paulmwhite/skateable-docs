What's new in Skateable

1.1 Coming soon
  • Onboarding New
    A guided first-run experience to get you set up quickly.
  • Location types New
    Categorise spots: skatepark, street spot, bowl, DIY, and more.
  • Location attributes New
    Tag spots with surface material, coverage, lighting, and other details that affect skateability.
  • Advanced filtering Improved
    Filter your saved locations by type, attributes, and current score.
  • Search Improved
    Faster, more relevant results when finding existing spots to save.
  • Location photos New
    Add photos to locations so others know what to expect before they visit.
  • Map view Improved
    Pan and zoom the map to explore locations near any point, not just your current position.
  • Weather reliability Improved
    Scores stay available even when the primary weather provider is down, with automatic fallback to a secondary source.

And much more.

March 2025

  • Skateability score New
    A 0–100 score based on current weather, 48h rain history, surface type, drainage, and sun exposure.
  • 7-day forecast New
    See a predicted score for sessions up to a week out.
  • Community reports New
    Skaters log whether a location was dry or wet, providing real ground truth.
  • No account needed New
    Save locations and log sessions immediately with no sign-up required.
  • Home screen widgets New
    Glanceable scores for your favourite spots without opening the app.
