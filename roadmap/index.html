Roadmap

Where we're headed

Up next In progress

Features actively being built for the next release.

  • Weather detail Tap the weather section on the Dashboard to see 48h rain history and forecast with hourly charts for precipitation, humidity, wind, and temperature.
  • Widget 2.0 The home screen widget gets descriptive condition labels and lets you pick which park to display directly from your home screen.
  • Session observations Tag conditions when logging (wet, crowded, closed) and mark a visit as a quick observation without a full session report. Recent community observations appear on the Dashboard.
  • Edit park details Correct a park's surface type, sun exposure, or drainage directly from the Dashboard. Changes update the skateability score immediately.
  • Community notes Approved observations from other skaters appear on the park Dashboard, so you know what to expect before you arrive.
  • Planned sessions Schedule sessions up to a week ahead, rank your preferred parks, and get notified when it's time to leave based on travel time and forecast conditions.
  • Proximity alerts Get a nudge to log a session or observation when you arrive near a saved park. Configurable in Settings with a cooldown to avoid repeat prompts.
  • Algorithm explanation A new Settings section explaining exactly how the skateability score is calculated, covering weather inputs, surface factors, and your tolerance offset.
  • iCloud sync Saved parks, favourites, settings, and planned sessions sync across your devices. Everything restores automatically on a fresh install.
  • Skateboarding Dryness Engine (SDE) A rebuilt scoring engine that produces more accurate skateability scores across different location types and conditions.
Requested Community

Features requested by the community. No timeline yet, but all are on our radar.

  • More photos Support for multiple photos per location, including community-contributed shots.
  • Get notified about changes Receive alerts when a saved location's details are updated, such as changes to surface, obstacles, or access, so your information stays current.
