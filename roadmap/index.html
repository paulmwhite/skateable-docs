Roadmap

Where we're headed

Up next In progress

Features actively being built for 1.3.

  • CarPlay See your favourite spots, live scores, and spot details on your car's display. Navigate to spots or parking and report surface conditions hands-free.
  • Offline caching Scores and recent sessions cached locally so CarPlay works when you lose signal. Queued reports sync when you reconnect.
  • Navigation app choice Choose Apple Maps, Google Maps, or Waze when navigating from CarPlay.
  • Widget engine migration Port the current scoring engine to Swift so widgets use the same engine as the app. Until then, widget scores may differ from in-app scores.
1.4 Planned

Features planned for 1.4.

  • Apple Watch Check scores for your favourite spots from your wrist. Glanceable complications and a lightweight companion app.
Requested Community

Features requested by the community. No timeline yet, but all are on our radar.

  • More photos Support for multiple photos per location, including community-contributed shots.
  • Get notified about changes Receive alerts when a saved location's details are updated, such as changes to surface, obstacles, or access, so your information stays current.
