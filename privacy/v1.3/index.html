Privacy Policy

Last updated: 4 April 2026

What we collect

  • Anonymous user ID: generated automatically on first launch via Supabase. No name, email, or account is required.
  • Locations you add: name and coordinates, stored in our database and readable by other users.
  • Sessions you log: dry/wet status, condition tags, optional notes, timestamp, and anonymous user ID.
  • Location flags: the flag reason, optional note, and anonymous user ID.
  • Photos: photos you attach to locations are uploaded to our servers and visible to other users. No EXIF or metadata beyond the image itself is stored.
  • Location (foreground): used to calculate distances, show nearby locations, and auto-fill coordinates when adding a location. Never sent to our servers.
  • Location (background): if you enable nearby spot alerts in Settings, your location is monitored in the background to notify you when you are near a saved spot. Background location data is processed on-device only and never sent to our servers.
  • Weather data: fetched from Open-Meteo, with automatic fallback to MET Norway (api.met.no), using your location coordinates. UK locations use MET Office data proxied via our servers. No personal data is sent with weather requests.
  • iCloud: saved spots, favourites, settings, and planned sessions are synced via Apple iCloud Key-Value Storage. This data is governed by Apple's iCloud terms and is not accessible to us.

What we don't collect

No name, email, phone number, device identifier, or usage analytics.

Data storage

Locations, sessions, flags, and photos are stored in Supabase hosted on AWS infrastructure. Anonymous user IDs are stored locally on your device. iCloud data is stored by Apple.

Data deletion

Tap Settings → Reset App to delete all locally stored data.

Third parties

  • Supabase (database, auth, and photo storage): supabase.com/privacy
  • Open-Meteo (weather): open-meteo.com/en/terms
  • MET Norway (weather fallback): met.no/en/About-us/privacy
  • MET Office (UK weather, proxied): metoffice.gov.uk/policies/privacy
  • Apple iCloud (sync): apple.com/legal/privacy
