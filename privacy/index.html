Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 April 2025

What we collect

  • Anonymous user ID — generated automatically on first launch via Supabase. No name, email, or account is required.
  • Parks you add — name and coordinates, stored in our database and readable by other users.
  • Sessions you log — dry/wet status, optional notes, timestamp, and anonymous user ID.
  • Park flags — the flag reason and anonymous user ID.
  • Location — used on-device only to calculate distances and show nearby parks. Never sent to our servers.
  • Weather data — fetched from Open-Meteo using your park coordinates. No personal data is sent.

What we don't collect

No name, email, phone number, device identifier, or usage analytics.

Data storage

Parks, sessions, and flags are stored in Supabase hosted on AWS infrastructure. Anonymous user IDs are stored locally on your device.

Data deletion

Tap Settings → Reset App to delete all locally stored data. To request deletion of server-side data (sessions, flags), email skateable@paulmwhite.com with the subject "Data deletion request".

Third parties

  • Supabase (database and auth) — supabase.com/privacy
  • Open-Meteo (weather) — open-meteo.com/en/terms
