Common questions

Why is my park showing the wrong score?
Scores are based on Open-Meteo weather data and community-logged sessions. If the score seems off, log a session (dry or wet) to correct it for other users. You can also adjust your personal tolerance in Settings.

How do I delete a park?
On the Locations tab, swipe left on the park and tap Unsave.

How do I delete my data?
Tap Settings → Reset App to clear all locally stored parks and settings. To request deletion of server-side data (sessions and flags), email skateable@paulmwhite.com with the subject "Data deletion request".

The app isn't loading weather data.
Check your internet connection. Weather is fetched from Open-Meteo on each screen load. If the problem persists, send us a feedback email from Settings — it includes logs automatically.

A park has incorrect information.
Swipe left on the park in the Locations tab and tap Flag, or use the flag icon on the Dashboard. Our team reviews flags and corrects bad data.
